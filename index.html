IYNA

| The next generation of neuroscientists |

Our mission is to ignite curiosity about neuroscience among high school students in our school and all around the globe. We dedicate our efforts to providing our candidates with guidance, resources, and atmosphere to engage in meaningful neuroscience endeavors. We aspire to establish an environment that promotes innovation, critical thinking, and self-development, enabling students to contribute to neuroscience. Our mission extends beyond classrooms, we strive to make neuroscience easy and interesting to learn. Through our annual issues, we target providing a torrential stream of articles and knowledge for our audience. This puts our juniors on the way to being eminent neuroscientists.
